# Diligence Room Index

## Open Diligence Lanes

- `vc-buyer-discovery` (product): 10 to 15 dated importer, broker, operator, or sourcing lead conversations. Next: Run buyer discovery and attach notes or no-fit evidence before claiming demand.
- `vc-design-partner` (founder): At least one written design-partner or pilot-intent artifact. Next: Convert warm operator interest into a dated pilot-intent or rejection note.
- `vc-compliance-review` (compliance): Qualified customs, food safety, and claims-language review. Next: Have a qualified reviewer inspect the country matrix and pitch claims.
- `vc-data-rights` (data): Source-rights, refresh cadence, and allowed-use proof for official and commercial sources. Next: Document data rights and repeatable refresh proof before scaling beyond fixtures.
- `vc-business-model` (founder): Pricing hypothesis, ICP, sales motion, and first 90-day experiment plan. Next: Validate willingness to pay during buyer discovery and update the pitch packet.
- `vc-legal-financing` (founder): Entity, IP, cap table, financing terms, and securities-law review. Next: Confirm fundraising terms with counsel before sending investor documents.
