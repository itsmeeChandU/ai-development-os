# Board Readiness Review Brief

## Review Goal

Review whether the product is ready for board or founder review of a controlled
private beta.

## Current Board Status

`board_go_live_candidate_with_human_approval_gates`

This means the product can be reviewed for a controlled private beta. It does
not mean public launch or production deployment approval.

## Files To Inspect

- `evidence/board_packet/board_go_live_brief.md`
- `evidence/board_packet/expert_review_packet.md`
- `evidence/board_packet/launch_control_checklist.md`
- `evidence/board_packet/financial_operating_model.md`
- `evidence/product_reports/board_go_live_readiness_report.json`
- `evidence/product_reports/continuation_plan.json`

## Questions

1. Should a controlled private beta be approved, rejected, or redirected?
2. Which human owners must be assigned before beta?
3. What controls are missing?
4. What support and rollback plan is required?
5. What should the beta scope include and exclude?
6. What evidence must be collected during beta?
7. What public-launch claims must stay closed?

## Recommended Decision Format

Use one of:

- approve controlled private beta with conditions
- reject private beta for now
- redirect to additional evidence gathering
- redirect to product redesign

Include the conditions and owners.
