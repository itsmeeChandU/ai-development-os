# Claim Boundaries And Open Gates

## Allowed Claims

The package may claim:

- the local product proof gate passes
- the internal operator app runs locally
- unsafe counters remain closed
- generated blocker and continuation reports exist
- Canadian reference tools are attached with boundaries
- a private investor conversation packet exists
- a board/private-beta review packet exists

## Not Allowed Claims

The package must not claim:

- public launch readiness
- production deployment approval
- buyer validation
- product-market fit
- revenue proof
- legal approval
- privacy approval
- financial advice approval
- customs/tariff advice readiness
- CFIA compliance approval
- import/export advice readiness
- supplier recommendation readiness
- signed contract readiness
- source/data rights approval

## Open Gates

The following require real external evidence:

- dated buyer/operator interviews
- qualified Canadian customs/trade/food compliance review
- legal/privacy review
- finance/founder/accountant review
- source rights and data freshness approval
- written contracts or terms
- restricted-party screening records
- security/ops deployment approval
- board or launch owner approval

## Why These Gates Stay Open

AI model subject synthesis can produce first-pass product logic, research
drafts, generated reports, and simulated expert-review lanes. It cannot prove
external-world truth, buyer demand, legal/compliance approval, customs/tariff
correctness, financial approval, signed contracts, or public launch readiness.
