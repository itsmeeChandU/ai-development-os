# Current Slice Vs Target Product

## Current Slice

Status: `development_complete_local_go_live_contract`

The current local product includes:

- beginner starter flow
- PDF quick check with quarantine, hashes, page count, text quality, document
  intelligence, OCR-required routing, and confirmation gate
- missing evidence and blocked-claim engine
- customer packet workspace
- report exports
- AI/no-AI policy artifacts
- redaction and manual-review fallbacks
- expert review packets and reviewer templates
- all-stage go-live runbook
- external review blocker ledgers
- AI-assisted simulated Wave 1 review
- local Docker/Compose shell and health endpoints
- final go-live decision artifacts

## Target Product

The target hosted product additionally requires real-world evidence:

- production identity, storage, database, logging, backup, monitoring, support,
  rollback, and incident response
- hosted upload sandboxing and malware scanning
- qualified privacy/legal/security review
- qualified trade-boundary/customs/CFIA/report-language review
- staged private-beta users and dated usability outcomes
- payment review before live checkout
- public go/no-go owner approval

## Correct Label
