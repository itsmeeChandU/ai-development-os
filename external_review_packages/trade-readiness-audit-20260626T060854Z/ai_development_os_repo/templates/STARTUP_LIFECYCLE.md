## Proof Boundary

AI can create first-pass product, research, R&D, screenshots, and operator
packets. Real buyer demand, legal/compliance approval, financial approval,
data rights, contracts, public launch approval, and qualified subject truth
remain evidence lanes or blockers until proven.
